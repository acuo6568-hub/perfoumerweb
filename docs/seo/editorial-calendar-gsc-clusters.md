# Weekly Editorial Calendar Mapped to GSC Query Clusters

## Objective

Create a consistent weekly publishing cadence tied to measurable GSC query clusters so each article has a clear ranking target and a conversion path.

## Execution Rules

- Publish 1 article per week (same day/time window).
- Submit each URL in Google Search Console within 24 hours.
- Add minimum 5 internal links from existing pages in first 48 hours.
- Link each article to at least 2 commercial pages (cluster or catalog).
- Track CTR and average position after 14 and 28 days.

## KPI Tracking (Weekly)

- GSC impressions for target query cluster
- GSC clicks and CTR for the new article URL
- Average position for primary keyword
- Assisted conversions from article to cluster pages
- Crawl/index status and canonical confirmation

## Content QA Checklist Before Publishing

- Title includes primary intent naturally.
- Meta description is unique and conversion-aware.
- Intro includes search intent and user problem.
- At least 3 practical sections + FAQ + CTA block.
- Internal links to 2-4 commercial pages.
- Article schema and FAQ schema present.
- Azerbaijani copy clarity checked for grammar and tone.
