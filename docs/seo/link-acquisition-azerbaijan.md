# Azerbaijan Link Acquisition Playbook (90 days)

## Goal

Build local authority and referral traffic for Perfoumer through high-relevance Azerbaijani mentions and backlinks.

## Core Principles

- Prioritize relevance over raw domain metrics.
- Build links to category and intent pages, not only homepage.
- Use natural anchors in Azerbaijani (brand + intent + neutral anchors).
- Track every outreach action with owner, status, and next follow-up date.

## Target Buckets

### 1) Azerbaijani directories and business listings

- Local business directories and shopping directories in Azerbaijan.
- Baku-focused commerce and city listing portals.
- Beauty/lifestyle vertical directories.

Target pages:

- /elaqe
- /haqqimizda
- /kisi-etirleri
- /qadin-etirleri

### 2) Local media and content collaborations

- Online magazines in lifestyle, beauty, and fashion.
- Local news portals with shopping/seasonal gift sections.
- Baku city guides and trend publications.

Asset ideas:

- Expert quote: seasonal perfume trends in Azerbaijan.
- Data story: "Most searched perfume intents in AZ 2026".
- Gift guide collaboration around holiday periods.

### 3) Influencer mentions (micro and mid-tier)

- TikTok and Instagram creators in beauty, lifestyle, and men grooming.
- Focus on creators with strong local trust and comment engagement.
- Prefer long-term monthly collaborations over one-off paid posts.

Campaign formats:

- "3 perfumes for office/weekend/date" reels.
- Note-focused education clips (oud, vanilla, citrus).
- Seasonal picks linked to cluster landing pages.

### 4) Brand and supplier collaborations

- Co-marketing with fragrance distributors and niche resellers.
- Brand profile features linking to /brands/[slug] pages.
- Joint campaign pages and reciprocal editorial mentions.

### 5) Lifestyle and niche sites

- Wedding and event planning blogs (gift/occasion intent).
- Men style and women beauty content websites.
- University/student lifestyle websites for budget fragrance guides.

## Outreach Workflow

1. Build a prospect list of 150 sites/accounts in 5 buckets.
2. Qualify each target by relevance, audience fit, and update cadence.
3. Prepare 3 content offers per bucket.
4. Send personalized outreach in Azerbaijani, follow up at day 4 and day 10.
5. Deliver content assets quickly and request dofollow editorial links where possible.
6. Track placements, anchors, destination URLs, and referral sessions.

## Anchor Strategy

Use a balanced mix:

- Branded: Perfoumer, Perfoumer.az
- Partial intent: "kişi ətirləri", "qadın ətirləri", "oud ətirlər"
- URL anchors: perfoumer.az/kisi-etirleri
- Natural neutral: "bu bələdçi", "bu səhifə", "mənbə"

Avoid repetitive exact-match anchors.

## KPI Dashboard (weekly)

- New referring domains (goal: +10 to +20/month)
- Follow links earned
- Referral sessions by source
- Rankings for core cluster keywords
- GSC clicks/impressions for linked pages
